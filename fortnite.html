Fortnite Page

About the game: Fortnite is an online multiplayer game where up to 100 players battle to be the last person standing on an island. Players gather resources, build structures, and fight while avoiding a shrinking storm. Its colorful graphics and regular updates have made it a cultural phenomenon.

  • Fortnite features multiple game modes, including Battle Royale, where up to 100 players compete to be the last one standing on an island.
  • Players can build structures and gather resources while avoiding a shrinking storm that damages those outside the safe zone.
  • The game is free-to-play, with in-game purchases available for cosmetic items and battle passes using the virtual currency called V-Bucks
